ABOUT ME

I was born in Madrid in 1990. My father's parents were painters and sculptors, and this made my childhood a very creative one.

Once I started high school, Science stole my heart at first sight. I graduated as an Environmental Scientist, although I started working in technological companies right after graduating, and fell in love with digital products. This took me to study UX/UI bootcamp at Ironhack in 2019.

As a former PA, I have developed myself as a multitasker, added to a multidisciplinary background which includes from Customer Service to analysis of scientific results. I see myself as a bold UX researcher, and I deem my approach to design as data inspired.

I'm very into Voice User Interface Design and currently pursuing to develop professionally in this unseen field.
